10.Modern family

"Modern Family" is a popular American television sitcom that originally aired from 2009 to 2020. Created by Christopher Lloyd and Steven Levitan, the show follows the lives of Jay Pritchett and his family, which includes his second wife, his stepson, and his two adult children and their families.

The series is presented in a mockumentary format, with characters speaking directly to the camera in interviews. The diverse and interconnected characters bring humor to the dynamics of a modern, blended family. The three main households featured in the show are led by Jay and his second wife, his daughter Claire and her family, and his son Mitchell and his partner.

"Modern Family" received critical acclaim for its humor, character development, and its representation of various family structures. It won numerous awards, including five Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Comedy Series consecutively from 2010 to 2014. The series concluded its run with its 11th season in 2020.

11.Never Have I Ever

"Never Have I Ever" is a coming-of-age comedy-drama television series created by Mindy Kaling and Lang Fisher. The show premiered on Netflix in April 2020. The story revolves around Devi Vishwakumar, played by Maitreyi Ramakrishnan, a first-generation Indian American teenager dealing with the challenges of adolescence while navigating her cultural identity.

After a tumultuous freshman year, Devi makes a New Year's resolution to improve her social standing, which leads to humorous and sometimes awkward situations. The show explores themes of grief, friendship, and the complexities of family relationships. It received positive reviews for its representation of diversity, cultural nuances, and its blend of humor and heartfelt moments. "Never Have I Ever" has been praised for its fresh take on the coming-of-age genre and its relatable storytelling.
